Peanut butter and jelly sandwich

Description

Peanut butter and jelly sandwiches couldn't be easier!

Ingredients

  • 2 slices bread
  • Peanut butter
  • Jelly

Steps

  1. Spread peanut butter on one slice of bread
  2. Spread jelly on the other slice of bread
  3. Bring the two slices of bread together, jelly and peanut butter facing inward
