Pizza

Description

Baking pizza isn't too hard, especially if you oversimplify the steps like we'll do in this example.

Ingredients

  • Pizza dough
  • Tomato sauce
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Basil

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 500 degrees Fahrenheit
  2. Stretch the pizza dough out in a baking sheet
  3. Spread a thin layer of tomato sauce on top
  4. Layer mozzarella cheese over the tomato sauce
  5. Bake pizza for 10 minutes
  6. Remove pizza from oven and garnish with basil
